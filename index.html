About Me


Systems engineer with more than 20 years of experience in technical and infrastructure areas linked to operations management and technology resource management teams. I have worked in IT and networks, coordination in operational areas and software training. In addition, I have worked in design of technological solutions in the area of Smart Home and Smart Building automation, certification and analysis of networks, channeling, structured cabling and fiber optics. Ensuring IoT interconnections.

As Project Engineer and Estimator, I have led automation projects of electronic security equipment, including infrastructure, design and migration of installed bases, service to suppliers and monitoring of projects in various sectors, such as industry, banks, newspapers, hotels, hospitals, universities and schools, data center and private homes.

In software design, I have collaborated with programmers for HMIWEB type projects taking digital signals to interact with equipment in the field and display their status in operator consoles, such as HVAC, Fire Panels, CCTV and Access Control.

In the development area, I have knowledge in Python, HTML and CSS. With which I have done small projects.
